Inventory Management Dashboard offers a range of practical tools designed to support accurate stock control, streamline inventory processes, and enhance operational visibility for businesses of all sizes.

Real-Time Stock Tracking

Monitor inventory levels across products and categories in real time, enabling better visibility and control over available stock at all times.

Automated Reorder Alerts

Define minimum stock thresholds and receive timely notifications when inventory reaches critical levels, helping to prevent shortages and disruptions.

Supplier Management

Maintain organised supplier records and associate suppliers with products to support faster purchasing decisions and improved supplier coordination.

Inventory Reports & Insights

Access clear and structured inventory reports, including low-stock summaries, stock movement trends, and high-demand product overviews.

Product Organisation

Categorise products by type, brand, or storage location to simplify searching, tracking, and day-to-day inventory management tasks.

Responsive & User-Friendly Design

Designed with usability in mind, the dashboard provides consistent navigation and responsive access across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.
